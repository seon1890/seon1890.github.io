About Me

My name is Seonhye Yang and I am a 2nd year M.S student studying Data Science at New York University. I am currently doing my capstone research in machine learning at the Flatiron Institute under Shirley Ho. My research interests expand from data science, human computer interaction, natural language processing, and psychology.

Prior to joining NYU, I earned a bachelors of science in statistics and psychology at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I was involved in various research labs including the Cascade lab, the Language Production Lab, and the Memory and Forgetting in Context lab.
